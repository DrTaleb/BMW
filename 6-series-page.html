The history of BMW 6 series

BMW E24 (1972)

Undoubtedly, the e24 was one of the best coupe cars in the world. With powerful and high-acceleration motors with low pollution due to the presence of intake manifold injection and the presence of catalytic converters

This model was designed by Paul Bracq. With its elongated and aggressive design, it was one of the most attractive coupes of that time, which has many fans nowadays

With the introduction of the BMW 635csi in 1978, BMW once again showed its power in upgrading its cars to competitors.With the upgrade of shock absorbers and amplifiers, this model received a lot of technical upgrades

In 1982 the BMW 6 series is re-engineered, In 1984, the BMW 635csi entered the market with the highest-end coupe in the world with the BMW m88 engine and changes to the brakes and assistance and the modified chassis. This model gives the driver a unique driving experience

bmw E63,E64 (2003)

BMW 6 Series became known as the Bavarian Shark from this generation onwards. The E63 is one of BMW's most distinctive products with its sleek, aggressive and borderless design. The integration of this chassis with 6 to 10 cylinder engines has made the car one of the fastest products of BMW. Those who have a BMW 6 Series of this generation can hardly convince themselves to change their car.

At the beginning of 2004, the BMW 6 Series Convertible entered the market. At the same time, the BMW m6 was launched with a 507-horsepower V10 engine

The 2007 update was accompanied by exterior design changes , specific overhaul of the controls and more efficient engines like a 4.8-liter 8-cylinder engine that powers the BMW 650i Coupé and BMW 650i Convertible.

bmw F06,F12,F13 (2011)

The third generation of the 6 series can be introduced as the most perfect generation of this series. Thanks to the perfect exterior design of Nader Faghihzadeh.
The BMW 6 Series Convertible in this generation has a fabric roof
The BMW 6 Series Gran Coupe has been available since 2012, emphasizing the spacious interior design.
The BMW M6 was one of the most memorable BMWs in this generation thanks to the 4.4 liter V8 engine with M TwinPower Turbo technology.

bmw G32 (2017)

The latest generation of the BMW 6 Series, excellent driving dynamics, passenger comfort and plenty of trunk space make this a comfortable and safe car to travel.

The 8-speed gearbox transmits power to the four wheels of this car, which in its 6-cylinder model accelerates it by 5 seconds.

This generation, like all new BMW series, has driver assistance systems such as crossing warnings and an emergency lane assistant. These systems, along with BMW's attractive interior design and lighting, give the driver and passengers a perfect riding experience.
