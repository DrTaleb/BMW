The history of BMW 3 series

Everything begins from E21 (1975)

The first generation of BMW 3 series introduced about 40 years ago with code E21.
A two door sedan!
in these days a two door sedan is a Contradiction but the BMW engineers weighed All aspects those days.
in year 1975 BMW dealerships was founded in the USA and this car has a big chance to have a good sale in the USA
totally 1.36 million E21 was made.

The first models of E21 was slow and heavy but after that BMW produced them with 6 cylinder injector engines and they had very special options like Limited slip differential and Gearbox with close ratios.

The E30 (1982)

This car can be called the most important car in the history of 3 Series
E30 unlike E21 was not only offered as coupe, it offered as coupe , sedan , five doors , convertible and touring.

The M3 E30

It was here that we saw the first diesel cars in the history of the 3 Series, also BMW's first M series mass-produced.

BMW 318is E30

BMW also unveiled versions with fewer options for people on a smaller budget, one of which was 318is.

The E46 (1998)

The release of E46 shows how valuable the Series 3 is to BMW
This generation had a very beautiful design and was improved in every direction compared to the previous series
Improvements in design, aerodynamics and strength were evident in this model

The E46 320d

In this generation of three diesel series, BMW showed its power to the world in designing high-acceleration and low-consumption diesel engines.
With zero to one hundred acceleration in less than 8 seconds and low fuel consumption, this generation had very good sales in Europe

The M3 E46

In this generation, the m3 returned to its former popularity despite failing in the previous generation

The M3 CSL

This model has improvements over the M3 including carbon fiber roof, SMG gearbox(For the first time in the history of BMW cars), 110 kg weight reduction, 16 hp more power and a stiffer suspension.
Only 1400 of these model were produced
