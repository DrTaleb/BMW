The history of BMW 2 series

(F22) BMW 2 series coupe (2013)

The BMW F22, a powerful successor to the 1 series Coupe, quickly gained popularity with its aggressive appearance. Up-to-date and original BMW design can be seen in this model. In 2017, with the change of rear lights and parts of the front of this model, it became more attractive.
A variety of agile engines from 1.5-liter to M-Series engines with this chassis create an engineered combination between power and handling.
In addition to chassis and engine engineering, in-car interior options like such as active cruise control, which reacts to the speed of the traffic in front of the vehicle. In addition, infotainment systems can make driving enjoyable and easier for the driver.

Production period 2013 – 2021

(F23) bmw 2 series convertible (2015)

The BMW 2 Series Convertible replaced the Series 1 Convertible in 2015. This model quickly settled in the hearts of BMW fans with improvements in design. This model has the ability to close the roof at a speed of 50 km per hour without any problems
This model is marketed with engines similar to the coupe.

(F44) BMW 2 series gran coupe (2020)

In 2020, BMW unveiled its new product called the BMW 2 Series Gran Coupe. New and aggressive design, three-dimensional grille and completely updated design and different from previous models, which shows a very beautiful and modern effect. This model with two 10.25-inch screens and head-up provides all the necessary information along the way to the driver

3 and 4-cylinder engines with 7 or 8-speed gearboxes were offered for this model.

(F45) bmw 2 series active tourer (2014)

If you want a car with a modern style but urban use, f45 is the best option for you, which can be the best choice for a family for urban use as well as for traveling. In standard mode, the three- to four-cylinder engines of this car drive the front wheels, but it can be purchased as an xdrive, which is four-wheel drive, and the rear wheels draw their power from an electric motor that can reach Travel 500 km

In 2018, the face-lifted model received an even more striking front and rear thanks to the redesigned lights, as well as updated engines with even lower emissions.

(G42) bmw 2 series coupe (2021)

In 2021, the BMW G42 was introduced as a compact sports car with high-performance , powerful engines. Due to its strange and different design compared to previous generations, it faced a lot of criticism, but in recent years, BMW has become accustomed to strange and unexpected designs and does not pay much attention to criticism.

With the BMW Digital Key your smartphone will be able to open and lock the vehicle or even start up the engine. And if you are ever unsure whether you have locked your car you can do so at any time while on the road via the My BMW app.
